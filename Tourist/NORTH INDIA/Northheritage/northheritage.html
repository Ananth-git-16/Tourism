TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is an iconic white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Completed in 1653, it combines Islamic, Persian, and Indian architecture, with intricate inlays calligraphy.
