DARJEELING

Darjeeling, referred to as the "Queen of the Hills," is a picturesque hill station in the Indian state of West Bengal. Nestled in the Himalayas, it is renowned for its breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, including Kanchenjunga, the world's third-highest mountain.

MAJULI

Majuli, the world's largest river island, is located in the Brahmaputra River in Assam, India. Known for its serene landscapes, lush greenery, and vibrant culture, it is a hub of Assamese art, culture, and spirituality. Majuli offers a peaceful retreat from urban life.

TAWANG

Tawang, a picturesque town in Arunachal Pradesh, India, is renowned for its stunning landscapes and spiritual significance. Nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, it is home to Tawang Monastery, one of the largest Buddhist monasteries in the world and a major pilgrimage site.

GANGTOK

Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, is a beautiful hill station nestled in the Eastern Himalayas. Known for its clean streets, vibrant culture, and breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga range, it offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and urban charm.
